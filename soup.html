Product Description

The gym duffel bag is a perfect reliable companion for both indoor and outdoor sports. It is a great bag for workout, travel, sports activity, tennis, basketball, yoga and many outdoor activities. Suitable for gym sport bag, travel duffel bag etc.

KEY FEATURES

SEPARATE SHOE COMPARTMENT

A separate shoe compartment can help you carry and keep your shoes better in your travel bag,even if it is dirty and the other objects still won't get stained.

PERFECT HOLDERS & OVERSIZED OPENING

Enough room with multiple compartments. Main compartment opens completely for easy packing and viewing. Left side pocket for sneakers. Right side pocket for minimal items, Front pocket for easy accessible material.

FOLDABLE & SUPER LIGHTWEIGHT

It can be folded into itself fast with a compact size of 23 x 26 x 4 only 1/20 of a regular size duffel bag. It weights only 400 gram, Comes with an adjustable shoulder strap.

TWO WAY CARRY STYLE

The Gym duffle bag has 2 handle straps and 1 adjustable shoulder strap you can carry it as handbag, shoulder bag or cross-body bag, it is up to you!
